President’s Address

Empowering the Future Together

Excerpt from the President’s Speech — 2025 Ipakodo Grammar School Reunion (Reconnect • Reflect • Rebuild)

It is a profound privilege to stand before you today as we gather once again on the grounds that shaped our early dreams, our values, and many of the lifelong relationships we still treasure.

This year’s theme, “Empowering the Future Together: Reconnect, Reflect, Rebuild,” captures the spirit of what Ipakodo Grammar School stands for — a community grounded in unity, wisdom, and progress.

As we reconnect with old classmates and teachers, we are reminded that no matter where life has taken us, this school remains our common foundation.

And now, it is time to rebuild — not because our school is broken, but because every strong community must continually evolve, supporting the next generation through mentorship, opportunity, and legacy.

Comrade Jaiyesimi Azeez (Makavelli) President, Ipakodo Grammar School 1998 Set Alumni
