From Virtual to Reality — And a New Era Begins

The dream became reality on November 22, 2025, at Paradox Bar & Lounge. The reunion — built on the foundation Alaran Bunmi created — brought us face-to-face after decades apart.

Today, the association continues to thrive under the strong, visionary leadership of Azeez Olalabi Jaiyesimi (Makaveli), our current President. Building on the pioneering spirit of Alaran Bunmi, he guides us forward with passion, unity, and purpose.
