From Alaran Bunmi
The Visionary Who Started It All

It all began with Alaran Bunmi, our revered Pioneer President — a man driven by memory, purpose, and an unshakable belief in the power of shared history.

He reached across time and distance — reconnecting classmates who had been scattered by life, careers, continents, and responsibilities.

As responses poured in, something remarkable happened. Familiar names reappeared. Laughter returned. Stories were shared. A sense of belonging was restored.

One message. One vision. A lifelong bond restored.

Building the Foundation

The WhatsApp group quickly became our heartbeat — laughter, support, planning, and purpose.

Through consistent leadership, trust was built and participation grew, turning nostalgia into action.

Together, the groundwork for something enduring was laid.

Azeez Olalabi Jaiyesimi

Current President

Leadership Today

Today, the association continues to thrive under the strong, visionary leadership of Azeez Olalabi Jaiyesimi (Makaveli), our current President.

Building on the pioneering spirit of Alaran Bunmi, he guides the association forward with passion, unity, and purpose — ensuring that progress is balanced with respect for our shared heritage.

The journey continues — and every member has a role to play in shaping the next chapter of our shared story.
