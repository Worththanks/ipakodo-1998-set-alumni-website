From Alaran Bunmi
The Visionary Who Started It All

It all began with Alaran Bunmi, our Pioneer President. One quiet evening, a single message brought scattered classmates back into one space.

That simple act revived friendships, restored shared identity, and laid the foundation for a formal alumni association.

One message. One vision. A lifelong bond restored.

The WhatsApp group quickly became our heartbeat — laughter, support, planning, and purpose.

Through consistent leadership, trust was built and participation grew, turning nostalgia into action.

Together, the groundwork for something enduring was laid.
