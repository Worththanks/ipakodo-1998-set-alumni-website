Board of Trustees

Guardians of Our Legacy

Esteemed alumni providing strategic guidance, oversight, and stewardship for the long-term sustainability of our association.

The Board of Trustees is composed of distinguished members of the 1998 Set who offer wisdom, experience, and unwavering commitment to our shared values. They serve as guardians of our legacy, providing high-level advisory oversight while the elected Executive Council manages day-to-day operations.

Together, they ensure transparency, constitutional adherence, and the continued growth of the Ipakodo Grammar School 1998 Set Alumni Association.
