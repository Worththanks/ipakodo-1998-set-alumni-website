Electoral Committee

Ensuring Fair Elections

Dedicated members responsible for conducting transparent, democratic elections within the association.

The Electoral Committee is an independent body tasked with overseeing the association's election process. Composed of impartial members, they ensure fairness, transparency, and adherence to the constitution during nominations, voting, and result declaration.
