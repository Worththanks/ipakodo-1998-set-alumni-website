Our Guiding Principles

Mission • Vision • Core Values

Anchored in our constitution, these principles define who we are and the legacy we build together.

Our Vision

To be a model alumni association renowned for unbreakable unity, exemplary leadership, and lasting positive impact on our members, alma mater, and society.

We envision a thriving, self-sustaining community where every member of the 1998 Set feels connected, supported, and proud — leaving an enduring legacy of excellence for future generations.

“A united, impactful, and enduring family that inspires pride and progress.”

“Reconnect • Reflect • Rebuild”

Our Mission

To foster lifelong connections among members of the 1998 Set, promote mutual welfare and development, contribute meaningfully to the growth of Ipakodo Grammar School, and serve as a force for good in society.

Through transparent governance, active engagement, and purposeful initiatives, we strengthen our bonds, support one another, and give back to the institution that shaped us.

Our Core Values

These values, enshrined in our constitution, guide every decision, action, and interaction within our association.

Unity

We stand together as one family, supporting and uplifting each other across boundaries and time.

Integrity & Transparency

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, accountability, and open governance in all we do.

Service & Welfare

We are committed to the well-being of our members and to giving back to our alma mater and community.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in leadership, initiatives, and impact — honouring the legacy of Ipakodo Grammar School.

Mentorship & Legacy

We guide the next generation and build sustainable structures that endure beyond our time.

Inclusivity

Every member’s voice matters. We welcome diversity and ensure all feel valued and heard.

These principles are formally enshrined in our association’s constitution.
