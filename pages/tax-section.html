Tax Reform Update — Key Points

  • • Taxes are mandatory and essential for national development
  • • Corporate Income Tax is payable by employers
  • • Revenue taxes are a major source of government funding
  • • FIRS collects all direct taxes
  • • Income below ₦800,000 annually is exempt from tax
  • • Higher-income earners will pay higher taxes
  • • Withholding tax increased by 20%
  • • Compensation for loss of office increased from ₦10m to ₦50m
  • • All taxpayers must obtain a valid TIN

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who is exempt from paying income tax?

Individuals earning below ₦800,000 annually are exempt under the new tax band.

Who pays Corporate Income Tax?

Employers and registered corporate entities are responsible for Corporate Income Tax.

Is a Taxpayer Identification Number mandatory?

Yes. All individuals and organisations must obtain a valid TIN.

Which body collects direct taxes?

The Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS) collects all direct taxes.
